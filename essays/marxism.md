### 马克思主义基本原理

---

#### [唯物史观](https://www.houlinzhi.com/essays/marxism/historical_materialism/index.html)：
1. 马克思为什么能够创立历史唯物主义？
2. 社会的内部结构和运动规律是什么？
3. 结合我国改革实际谈谈唯物史观的方法论意义。

#### [政治经济学](https://www.houlinzhi.com/essays/marxism/political_economy/index.html)：
1. 马克思为什么能够创立科学的政治经济学？
2. 马克思的劳动价值论的本质和内涵是什么？
3. 结合中国改革实际分析如何正确利用价值规律在
   经济发展中的积极作用，避免其消极作用。
4. 为什么说剩余价值理论揭示了资本主义经济
   的本质和资本家剥削的秘密？

#### [资本主义](https://www.houlinzhi.com/essays/marxism/capitalism/index.html)：
1. 怎样评价资本主义在人类历史上的作用？
2. 如何看待资本主义新变化中的进步因素和潜在危机？
3. 资本主义必然被社会主义所取代的根据是什么？
4. 如何正确认识和处理当代社会主义与资本主义的关系？

#### [社会主义和共产主义](https://www.houlinzhi.com/essays/marxism/socialism/index.html)：
1. 社会主义与资本主义的本质区别是什么？
2. 如何看待社会主义对人类文明发展的作用？
3. 共产主义实现的历史必然性何在？
4. 苏东剧变的历史教训和启示有哪些？
5. 中国特色社会主义有哪些制度优势？

#### 重点思考题（小论文 1500字左右）

1. 论社会基本矛盾原理对我国改革的指导意义
2. 论充分运用价值规律推动我国经济发展
3. 论社会主义与资本主义的本质区别
4. 论正确认识和处理社会主义与资本主义关系

#### Historical Materialism:
1. Why was Marx able to establish historical materialism?
2. What is the internal structure and the law of motion of society?
3. Discuss the methodological significance of historical materialism in relation to China's reform reality.

#### Political Economics:
1. Why was Marx able to create a scientific political economy?
2. What is the essence and connotation of Marx's labor theory of value?
3. Analyze how to correctly utilize the value law in economic development based on China's reform reality, and how to avoid its negative effects.
4. Why does the theory of surplus value reveal the essence of the capitalist economy and the secret of capitalist exploitation?

#### Capitalism:
1. How do we evaluate the role of capitalism in human history?
2. How should we view the progressive factors and potential crises in the new changes of capitalism?
3. What is the basis for the inevitable replacement of capitalism by socialism?
4. How should we correctly understand and handle the relationship between contemporary socialism and capitalism?

#### Socialism and Communism:
1. What is the essential difference between socialism and capitalism?
2. How do we view the role of socialism in the development of human civilization?
3. Where lies the historical inevitability of the realization of communism?
4. What are the historical lessons and insights of the Eastern European and Soviet dramatic changes?
5. What are the institutional advantages of socialism with Chinese characteristics?

#### Key Reflective Essays (approximately 1500 words)
1. On the guiding significance of the principle of social fundamental contradictions for China's reform
2. On fully utilizing the value law to promote China's economic development
3. On the essential difference between socialism and capitalism
4. On correctly understanding and handling the relationship between socialism and capitalism
